Jojo part 2

Set in 1938, "Battle Tendency" follows Joseph Joestar, grandson of Jonathan Joestar. Joseph discovers his Ripple (Hamon) ability and battles the Pillar Men: ancient superhumans seeking the Red Stone of Aja for ultimate power. With allies like Caesar Zeppeli and mentor Lisa Lisa, Joseph trains worldwide to master Ripple techniques. The arc culminates in intense battles against the Pillar Men, showcasing Joseph's cunning and courage as he thwarts their ambitions.

Jojo part 1

Set in 19th-century England, "Phantom Blood" follows Jonathan Joestar and his adopted brother, Dio Brando. Dio schemes to usurp the Joestar fortune using an ancient stone mask that turns him into a vampire. Jonathan learns Hamon (Ripple) from Will A. Zeppeli to combat Dio's undead minions. The conflict culminates in a final battle where Jonathan sacrifices himself to defeat Dio and save humanity, setting the stage for the epic "JoJo" saga.
