About the
creator

Hirohiko Araki is a highly regarded Japanese manga artist born on June 7, 1960, in Sendai, Japan. He is best known as the creator of the long-running and influential manga series "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure," which has been serialized since 1987. Araki's career began in the early 1980s, and he quickly gained recognition for his unique artistic style and imaginative storytelling. His art is characterized by intricate linework, dynamic poses, and a keen attention to detail. Araki often incorporates elements of fashion, history, and mythology into his narratives, creating a blend that is both visually striking and intellectually engaging.
